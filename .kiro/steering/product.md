# Product Overview

Greater Works City Church (GWCC) Management Dashboard - A comprehensive church management system for tracking members, attendance, donations, events, sermons, and administrative operations.

## Core Purpose

Centralized platform for church administrators and staff to manage all aspects of church operations including member records, event planning, financial tracking, and communication.

## Key Features

- **Member Management**: Track member profiles, contact info, ministries, demographics, and membership status
- **Attendance Tracking**: Record and analyze attendance for services and events
- **Donation Management**: Process and track tithes, offerings, and special donations with receipt generation
- **Event Planning**: Schedule and manage church events, services, and activities
- **Sermon Library**: Archive sermons with media files, transcripts, and scripture references
- **Announcements**: Publish and manage church-wide communications
- **Reports & Analytics**: Generate insights on attendance trends, giving patterns, and membership growth
- **Ministry Groups**: Organize and track ministry participation and leadership

## Target Users

- Church administrators
- Staff members
- Ministry leaders
- Finance team

## Technical Approach

Progressive Web App (PWA) with offline capabilities, mobile-responsive design, and real-time data synchronization via Supabase backend.
